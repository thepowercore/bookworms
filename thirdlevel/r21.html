What is this book about?


It is about a baby who is very talented and extremely clever. The main characters are baby George and his seven year old sister named Laura. They get on very well together. They share a special secret.




What did you particularly enjoy about this book?

The part I liked the best was his first birthday party.


What did you dislike about this book?


I disliked the ending because I wanted the book to be a bit longer. I feel it finished a bit too soon.


In summary, who do you think might like this book? Is there a particular age range it’s best suited to? Are there other books or series you think it’s similar to?


I think around 7 or 8 year olds would like this book.
This book reminded me of a book I read called "Baby Brains" by Simon James. You can also get it in Irish: "Micí Meabhrach".


An overall mark out of ten

8.5
