What is this book about?


Fairy pony princess Rosabel comes to visit the fairy ponies on Fairy Pony Island. Holly and her pony friend Puck are given the important job of minding her, but then she goes missing so they need the help of all the fairy ponies to find her. Evil Shadow has kidnapped her and wants to take her throne. It’s a race to rescue her before he succeeds.
Join Holly and Puck on this exciting adventure through Fairy Pony Island as they try to make up for losing Princess Rosabel.




What did you particularly enjoy about this book?

I thought this book was very interesting because the main characters managed to save the day by helping even though they were told not to by the fairy ponies. Overall it was a very enjoyable read.


What did you dislike about this book?


I didn’t like one of the characters, Princess Rosabel, as she was very bossy and rude.


In summary, who do you think might like this book? Is there a particular age range it’s best suited to? Are there other books or series you think it’s similar to?


I think girls who like adventure stories would like this book. I think it’s suitable for age 7-9.
