What is this book about?


Emil and the Detectives is about a young boy called Emil from Neustadt, a small town in Germany, who has to travel by himself to Berlin on a train. He is carrying money which his grandmother has asked him to deliver to his aunt in Berlin. While he is asleep on the train an adult stranger steals the money. Emil travels on to Berlin in pursuit of the stranger. In Berlin he meets Gustav and his gang of streetwise friends. Gustav and his gang join Emil in his quest to get the money back and so a great adventure begins!




What did you particularly enjoy about this book?

I liked ‘Emil and the Detectives’ is an adventure with a lot of twists and turns. I didn’t know how the story would end. I liked that Emil, Gustav and his friends were about my age and they have to succeed in their quest they have to be smarter than the adults around them.
I liked Emil. At the beginning of the book he appears a bit innocent and gullible but by the end of the book he has proved himself to be pretty streetwise.


What did you dislike about this book?


There was nothing that I didn’t like about Emil and the Detectives but I wish that it was part of a series as I would like to read more of Emil’s adventures.


In summary, who do you think might like this book? Is there a particular age range it’s best suited to? Are there other books or series you think it’s similar to?


I think readers who like stories where children are the heroes and have to outsmart adults to overcome challenges will like this book. It is a short book and quite easy to read.
