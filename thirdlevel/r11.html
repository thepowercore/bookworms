What is this book about?


Brendon Chase is about three brothers who run away from their aunt, who has been looking after them while their parents are away. The boys are sick of being treated like babies by their strict and over-protective aunt. On the day before their return to boarding school the boys slip out of the house and go to live in a forest named Brendon Chase. The book tells the story of the year that the boys spend living wild and free in the forest. They have many exciting and funny adventures in the forest.




What did you particularly enjoy about this book?

I liked ‘Brendon Chase’ because it is about boys who are about my age. They live wild and free in the outdoors, shooting and fishing to survive.
I think that most children my age like the idea of living free in the great outdoors. It is really exciting to read about the boys’ life in the forest with days full of swimming, climbing, hunting and fishing, away from school and parents and free from rules.


What did you dislike about this book?


I didn’t like that the adventure had to end and that the boys eventually had to return home. I wish they had just stayed in the forest.


In summary, who do you think might like this book? Is there a particular age range it’s best suited to? Are there other books or series you think it’s similar to?


‘Brendon Chase’ is a great book. Readers who love adventure and the great outdoors will enjoy this book. It is pretty long and some of the language is difficult so readers 9 and over would enjoy it.
