What is this book about?


Joe Spud is an 11-year-old and also a billionaire with lots of toys. Joe has every thing but he realises that Olympic-sized swimming pools and plasma TVs is not what he really wants. What he really wants is a friend or two. After forcing his Dad to agree, Joe starts in a public school to try and find a normal friend.




What did you particularly enjoy about this book?

I liked that he that Joe doesn’t care about the money all he wants is a friend.


What did you dislike about this book?


He was mean to his dad when his dad was trying to help.
