What is this book about?


The book is about a boy called Johnny who worked in the savoy hotel as a bell boy. One day a Countess from Russia came to stay in the hotel and it was Johnny’s job to do everything she asked – she had a cat called Kaspar.
The arrival of the Countess and Kasper changed his life for ever, Kasper was a very special cat, and was “the most beautiful cat in the whole world” Johnny and Kasper become special friends and meet another little girl called Elizabeth who came to stay in the hotel with her parents.
The book then goes on to describe the great adventure they have in getting to New York on the Titanic!




What did you particularly enjoy about this book?

I loved the way author describes the journey on the Titanic. I also loved the boy Johnny. It is a really enjoyable book that was easy to read and very interesting and exciting.

Nothing.
