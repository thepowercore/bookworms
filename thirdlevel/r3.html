What is this book about?


The Magic Faraway Tree Collection has the complete 3 books of the faraway tree adventures. Book 1 is The Enchanted Wood, Book 2 is the Magic Faraway Tree and Book 3 is The Folk of the Faraway Tree.
It is about 3 children, who lived in a town all of their lives and they moved to the country when their Father got a new job. At first they didn’t think they would have much fun in the country but then they found a forest beside their house and in the forest there was a magic tree.
They meet lots of interesting and fun friends who live in the tree, and have great adventures. They meet Moon-Face, Saucepan Man and a lovely fairy called Silky.
The tree is amazing and you can’t believe all the things that happen in the tree – there is even a slide going down the centre of the tree!! All these little people live in the tree and become great friends with the children.
At the top of the tree there were clouds and when you climbed through the clouds there was always a different land there full of surprises. Some lands were very exciting and fun and some were very dangerous.
