About This Website

This website is to help people looking for advice on what sort of books to choose. I have had that same problem myself, and that is why I decided that I should code this website. This is for children 8-12 years old, searching for books. And it can be really hard looking at a blurb and trying to figure out whether you should buy a book or not, cause blurbs have an annoying tendnecy not to tell you too much. And if you head onto a common-or-garden review website, most of the reviews are done by adults, so it's really not the same.
